Hi there,

I'm James Bennett. I love photography, weddings, and coffee and I'd love to be your photographer. I appreciate taking the time to wait for a sunset to be just right. My favorite moment in a wedding is when the bride and groom walk down the isle and see each other for the very first time. Seeing those smiles always brings me back to my wedding day when I first saw my beautiful wife. I'll take the time to make sure your husband's shirt is just right. Geeking out on new gear and lighting techniques is a favored pastime. I will even dance at your wedding, I secretly love to dance.

My style is somewhere between editorial and wedding lifestyle. I take inspiration from fashion and fine art photography. I'm very comfortable just using my Nikon and a single 50mm lens. My first wedding I pretty much shot the whole thing on that. Although, if you ask I'll bring out the pro lighting and go Annie Leibovitz on you.

My job is to tell your story. I'll make sure to catch ever tear, ever laugh, and even your best friend's amazing dance skills. I am here to help document and create lasting memories of your special day and can't wait to work with you! I look forward to hearing from you soon.
